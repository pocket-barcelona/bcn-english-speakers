## Visiting Cadaqués

Cadaqués is one of the most secluded spots on the Costs Brava and is incredibly photogenic. Characterized by its white buildings and cobbled streets, it’s a popular choice for holidaymakers, visitors to the area and romantic getaways. It is best reached by car, although do allow extra time when passing Roses as the road snakes up and through the mountains.

It can also be reached by speedboat from Roses or L’Escala directly as well as other places close by.

The place is small and quaint and so doesn’t need a huge explanation - just walk around and explore.
