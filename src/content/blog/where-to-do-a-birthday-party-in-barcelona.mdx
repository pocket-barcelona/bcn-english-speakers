## Where to host a birthday party in Barcelona

Finding a cool activity or place for your birthday party in Barcelona can be tricky and these days it's hard to be original. Having had the same problem many times, I decided to write down all the things when planning previous parties in Barcelona!

Some things below are in general quite good and you won't need an excuse to do them!
